## **School of Transmutation**

### “Water to wine”

Casters who study within the School of Transmutation are able to manipulate the physical properties of both items and people. This could be something simple – such as turning copper into gold – or could be an advanced spell that turns you into a newt (_it’ll get better…)_

Examples:

> **Low Level**: _Shape Water, Feather Fall_
> 
> **Mid Level**: _Gaseous Form, Stone Shape_
> 
> **High Level**: _Polymorph, Etherealness_
