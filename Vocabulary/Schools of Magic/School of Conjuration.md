## **School of Conjuration**

### “Pulling a rabbit from a hat”

The School of Conjuration deals with creating objects and creatures, or making them disappear. 

Examples:

> **Low Level**: _Find Familiar, Poison Spray_
> 
> **Mid Level**: _Spirit Guardians, Conjure Elemental_
> 
> **High Level**: _Plane Shift, Wish_

_DM Thoughts: You might think Banishment would be within the School of Conjuration, since it deals with making things disappear, but it’s actually within the School of Abjuration._
