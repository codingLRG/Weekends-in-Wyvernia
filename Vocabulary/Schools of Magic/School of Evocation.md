## **School of Evocation**

### “KAMEHAMEHA!!”

Casters within the school of evocation unleash a raw magical energy upon their enemies. Whether it be flames, ice, or pure arcane energy: evocation spellcasters are here to deal damage and chew gum… and they’re all out of gum. 

Examples:

> **Low Level**: _Burning Hands, Dancing Lights_
> 
> **Mid Level**: _Fireball, Freezing Sphere_
> 
> **High Level**: _Meteor Swarm, Prismatic Spray_

_Unsolicited DM Thoughts: The spell sending is within the School of Evocation, though I think it is a better fit for the School of Divination._
