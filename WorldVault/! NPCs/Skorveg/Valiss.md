Their wealth, power, influence, and overall presence can be traced back 500 years or so. Almost overnight as a matter of fact it seems they rose to high status. A family of powerful “sorcerers”, a bloodline personally crafted by the “gods”. All a lie. The truth was, [[Valiss]], the original patriarch of the family made a deal with the devil. Forever damning the family to wealth, power and servitude.

All these years later [[Alice|Sylvy]] with her closest siblings were assigned to get rid of an opposing cult and during the attack on the opposing cult’s head, her older brother [[Ciel]] died. Heartbroken and in disarray [[Alice|Sylvy]] decided to leave her family home.
