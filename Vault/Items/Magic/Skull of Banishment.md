# Skull of Banishment

> Very Rare, A seemingly ordinary human skull with near perfect wear

# Magical Properties

| School of Magic                      | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[School of Abjuration\|Abjuration]] | When a sentient creature touches foreheads with the skull, a [Banishment](https://5e.tools/spells.html#banishment_xphb) Charisma DC 14 is cast on the creature, if the skull is already inhabited, the creature inside is released. The spell only ends if the skull is destroyed. Creatures who are touching the skull can freely communicate with the banished creature telepathically. |
