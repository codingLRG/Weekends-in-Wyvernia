> Grand Split: City of Clay and Glass

One of [[Wyvernia]]'s best supplier of masonry, the city is constructed with stone brick building. The mayor [[Fillbottom, Mayor of the Grand Split]] is a man of no adventuring accomplishments, however he has a very jolly personality and is very involved in the community. Being a river city, the power provided by waterwheels drives the craftsmen's tools to construct their merchandise.
