> Moore: City of Power and Riches

The most powerful capital in all of [[Wyvernia]], it was seen as the victor of the Southern [[Wyvernia]] war that prompted the [[Age of Conflict]]. Lead by general [[Talabut, King of Moore]], he launched the ultimate weapons project: [[Project Colossus]], that devastated all other existing capitals and enforced an era of peace. In a dormant volcano, precious stones are frequently mined, none more precious than [[Ember Heart]], none more useful than [[Dwarven Steel]].
