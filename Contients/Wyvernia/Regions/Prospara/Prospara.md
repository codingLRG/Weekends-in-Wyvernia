> Prospara: The Capital of Gold

One of Northern [[Wyvernia]]'s oldest civilization, a city built of yellow bricks and a capital central with a golden statue of [[Waukeen, goddess of trade]]. The leader [[Tostira, King of Prospara]] is a powerful alchemist wizard with a knack for transmutation, and has a strong understanding of economics. Rumors state that he is able to turn the sand of [[Apostos]] into gold. [[Prospara]] has earned its name as there has been no recorded time of economic hardship, even during times of rebellion and war.
