> Ta'alaki: Capitals of Thieves

[[Wyvernia]]'s island civilization, housed to Orcs and other ferocious individuals, [[Ta'alaki]] is a city where the structures are of ruined ships and hideouts. The leader [[Grungo the Ugly, Pirate King]] has not been seen in ages, instead sending his decrees through a magical skull that he communicates through, located in [[Dead Man's Quarters]]. Those who have disobeyed have found their ships added to the island not too long after. The island's 3 pirate lords are [[Dread Pirate Roberto]], [[Niketas O'Neill]], and [[Vorus Greybeard]], the only pirates with the right to commune with [[Grungo the Ugly, Pirate King]].
