# Winged Ammunition

Ranged weapon attack rolls made with this ammunition ignore half and three-quarters cover. In addition, attacking at long range doesn't impose disadvantage on ranged weapon attack rolls made with this ammunition. Base items. This item variant can be applied to the following base items:Arrow (Winged Arrow)Bolt (Winged Bolt)Firearm Bullet (Winged Firearm Bullet)Needle (Winged Needle)Sling Bullet (Winged Sling Bullet)
