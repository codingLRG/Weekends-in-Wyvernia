# Mithral Armor

Mithral is a light, flexible metal. A mithral chain shirt or breastplate can be worn under normal clothes.If the armor normally imposes disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks or has a Strength requirement, the mithral version of the armor doesn't. Base items.
