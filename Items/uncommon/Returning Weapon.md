# Returning Weapon

This magic weapon grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it, and it returns to your hand immediately after you use it to make a ranged attack roll. Base items. This item variant can be applied to the following base items:Dagger (Returning Dagger)Dart (Returning Dart)Handaxe (Returning Handaxe)Javelin (Returning Javelin)Light Hammer (Returning Light Hammer)Spear (Returning Spear)Trident (Returning Trident)
