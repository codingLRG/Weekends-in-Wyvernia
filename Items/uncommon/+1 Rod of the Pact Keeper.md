# +1 Rod of the Pact Keeper

While holding this rod, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your Warlock spells.In addition, you can regain one spell slot as a Magic action while holding the rod. You can't use this property again until you finish a Long Rest.
