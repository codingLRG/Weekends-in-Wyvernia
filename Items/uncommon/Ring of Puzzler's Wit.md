# Ring of Puzzler's Wit

This gold ring bears a fluorite stone and is enchanted to sharpen the wearer's mind.The ring has 3 charges and regains 1d4 - 1 expended charges daily at dawn. When you make an Intelligence check, you can expend 1 charge to grant yourself advantage on the check.
