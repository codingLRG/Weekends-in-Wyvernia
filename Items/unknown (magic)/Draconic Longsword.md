# Draconic Longsword

This longsword has a dragon-shaped hilt. While you carry it, you gain the ability to speak and understand the Draconic language. Versatile. This weapon can be used with one or two hands. A damage value in parentheses appears with the property—the damage when the weapon is used with two hands to make a melee attack.
