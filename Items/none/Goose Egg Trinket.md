# Goose Egg Trinket

Sometimes, an egg laid by a giant goose inexplicably contains some kind of small trinket or minor magic item. Once it lays a golden egg, a giant goose can't do so again for a year and a day.You can use the Goose Egg Trinket table to determine the contents of a golden egg.
