# Sword of Life Stealing

When you attack a creature with this magic weapon and roll a 20 on the d20 for the attack roll, that target takes an extra 15 Necrotic damage if it isn't a Construct or an Undead, and you gain Temporary Hit Points equal to the amount of Necrotic damage taken. Base items. This item variant can be applied to the following base items:Glaive (Glaive of Life Stealing)Greatsword (Greatsword of Life Stealing)Longsword (Longsword of Life Stealing)Rapier (Rapier of Life Stealing)Scimitar (Scimitar of Life Stealing)Shortsword (Shortsword of Life Stealing)
