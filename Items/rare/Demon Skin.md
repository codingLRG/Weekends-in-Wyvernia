# Demon Skin

This magic armor appears as a pot of bubbling black ichor. When you attune to it, the ichor adheres to and contours to your skin, and the pot disappears. The armor can be worn under normal clothes, and it doesn't impede bodily functions. Once you put it on, it can't be removed unless you choose to do so or you die, at which point the pot reappears and the ichor flows back into it.While wearing the armor, you have resistance to poison damage. The armor also doesn't impose disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. Base items. This item variant can be applied to the following base items:Chain Mail (Demon Skin Chain Mail)Plate Armor (Demon Skin Plate Armor)Ring Mail (Demon Skin Ring Mail)Splint Armor (Demon Skin Splint Armor)
