# Ring of Animal Influence

This ring has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can expend 1 charge to cast one of the following spells (save DC 13) from it:Animal FriendshipFear (affects Beasts only)Speak with Animals
