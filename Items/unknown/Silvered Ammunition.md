# Silvered Ammunition

Some monsters that have immunity or resistance to nonmagical weapons are susceptible to silver ammunition, so cautious adventurers invest extra coin to plate their ammunition in silver. You can silver ten pieces of ammunition for 100 gp. This cost represents not only the price of the silver, but the time and expertise needed to add silver to the ammunition without making it less effective. Base items. This item variant can be applied to the following base items:Arrow (Silvered Arrow)Bolt (Silvered Bolt)Firearm Bullet (Silvered Firearm Bullet)Needle (Silvered Needle)Sling Bullet (Silvered Sling Bullet)
