Temporal Strike (Recharge 6). Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 52 (8d12) psychic damage. When the effect ends, the target reappears in the space it left or in an unoccupied space nearest to that space if it's occupied.
