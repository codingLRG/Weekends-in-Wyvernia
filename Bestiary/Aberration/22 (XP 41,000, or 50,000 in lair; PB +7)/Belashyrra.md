The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.4. Blinding Ray. The target and each creature within 10 feet of it must succeed on a DC 22 Constitution saving throw or take 19 (3d12) radiant damage and be blinded for 1 minute.
