### Main Actions
Multiattack. The slaad makes three Chaos Staff attacks.

Spellcasting. The slaad casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks):At will: Detect Magic, Detect Thoughts, Mage Hand1/day each: Fireball, Invisibility (self only)
### Bonus Actions
