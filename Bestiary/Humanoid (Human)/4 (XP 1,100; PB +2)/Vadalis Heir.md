## Actions
### Main Actions
Multiattack. The heir makes four attacks, using Scimitar or Lunar Wisp in any combination.
Scimitar. Melee Attack Roll: +5, reach 5 ft. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) Slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) Poison damage.
Lunar Wisp. Ranged Attack Roll: +5, range 60 ft. Hit: 9 (2d8) Radiant damage, and the target emits Dim Light in a 10-foot radius and can't benefit from the Invisible condition until the end of the heir's next turn.
Spellcasting. The heir casts one of the following spells, using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13):At will: Druidcraft, Light, Speak with Animals3/day each: Animal Friendship, Animal Messenger1/day: Ice Storm
