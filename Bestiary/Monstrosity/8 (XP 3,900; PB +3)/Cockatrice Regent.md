### Main Actions
Multiattack. The cockatrice makes one Petrifying Bite attack and two Talons attacks.
Petrifying Bite. Melee Attack Roll: +7, reach 5 ft. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) Piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it is subjected to the following effect. Constitution Saving Throw: DC 14. Second Failure: The target has the Petrified condition instead of the Restrained condition.
