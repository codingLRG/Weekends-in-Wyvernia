### Main Actions
Multiattack. The sorrowsworn makes two Arm Spike attacks.

Embrace (Recharge 4–6). Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (4d10 + 3) piercing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 14) if it is a Medium or smaller creature.
