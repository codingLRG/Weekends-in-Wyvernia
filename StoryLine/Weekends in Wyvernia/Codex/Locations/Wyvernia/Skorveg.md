> Skorveg: Capital of Fish and Beer

The most recently developed capital, it's construction marked a new and current age, the [[Age of Discovery]]. Buildings of damp wood, with the only building, surrounding the capital is a wall of marble. The leader [[Donovan]] was the first adventurer to successfully traverse [[The Great Black]] and come back with the discovery of a new continent, [[Estova]]. Now seen as the launching for modern adventurers as Skorveg is the only capital with the knowledge how to traverse [[The Great Black]].
